Facilities

Centre for Career Development is well equipped with excellent infrastructure to support each and every stage of the placement processes in the campus. The pre-placement talks rooms, group discussion rooms are equipped with LCD projectors, PCs and audio-video facilities. Centre for Career Development, IIT Guwahati has sufficient number of interview rooms.

IITG guesthouse has all modern facilities. All the rooms are air-conditioned and have Internet connectivity. We charge a nominal amount from guests on official visits. Both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes are available on order. Food bills are to be completely borne by the guests. Apart from many guesthouses, Guwahati city has a plenty of budget hotels including four-star hotels. Transport will be available from the hotels to the campus.

The auditorium is a large space with a capacity of seating 1500 individuals. It has excellent audio and projector systems to host a variety of events and live performances giving an immersive experience.

IIT Guwahati has 4 conference halls, each having a capacity of seating 150 individuals. Conference halls are equipped with state of the art technology to host live webinars and interactive guest lectures.

The Computer & Communication Centre, IIT Guwahati has a seating capacity of up to 200. It houses high-end computers capable of handling the latest software. These systems are regularly used to conduct on-campus recruitment tests.
