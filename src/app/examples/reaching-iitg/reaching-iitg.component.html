Reaching IITG

Location

  • Guwahati lies at the heart of Assam state and is the largest city in North-East India
  • IIT Guwahati campus lies just north of main Guwahati city (separated by the Brahamputra river)
  • The campus is at half an hour drive from the main city (15 km distance)

Connectivity

  • Guwahati is well connected to other parts of the country by road, rail and air
  • The city has good cab service available from Airport and Railway Station
  • Cab services like Ola and Uber are readily available in Guwahati
  • The institute also has a bus service that runs regularly to and from the city

Guwahati Airport

  • Direct flight to all metro/major cities
  • Distance between IIT Guwahati and Airport : 23 km
  • Time to reach IIT Guwahati from Airport : roughly 30 mins
  • Approx cab fare : INR 500
